Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from tourists and operators.

My visa has expired. What should I do immediately? â–¼
Contact FRRO immediately at 1800-11-1363 or visit indianfrro.gov.in. Do not attempt to leave India without regularising your status â€” you could be detained at the airport. Carry your passport, all visa documents, hotel bookings, and financial proof to the nearest FRRO office. Overstaying is an offence under the Foreigners Act 1946, but regularisation is possible.
How do I apply for an Indian e-Tourist Visa? â–¼
Apply online at indianvisaonline.gov.in. The e-Tourist Visa is available for 169 countries, with processing in 3â€“5 business days. Validity options: 30 days, 1 year, or 5 years. Entry is permitted only through 28 designated international airports. Fee ranges from $25 to $80 depending on duration.
How do I register as a verified tour operator on Yatra? â–¼
Register on the platform as an "Operator" and upload your Ministry of Tourism (MoT) operator licence, GST certificate, and PAN card. Our team verifies credentials within 48 hours. Once approved, you receive a "MoT Verified" badge and access to tourist lead management tools.
What languages does the Tourist Helpline (1363) support? â–¼
The National Tourist Helpline 1363 supports English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, and more. It operates 24/7 and can route calls to local state tourism boards for region-specific assistance.
How does the Yatra visa tracker work? â–¼
When you register with your visa details (expiry date, visa type, entry date), our system tracks your remaining days. You receive automatic alerts 30 days, 7 days, and 1 day before expiry. If your visa expires without a registered departure, the system flags your account as "Overstaying" and connects you with FRRO guidance immediately.
Is my personal data safe on the Yatra platform? â–¼
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We comply with the Ministry of Electronics & IT data protection guidelines. Passport and visa data is used solely for visa tracking and is never sold to third parties. You can request data deletion at any time by emailing privacy@yatra.gov.in.
